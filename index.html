About Morning Coffee Thoughts

Personal reflections over a cup of coffee. Thoughts on life, the Philippines, and everything in between.

Morning Coffee Thoughts (MCT) is an independent Philippine political commentary platform that covers the issues that matter most to Filipinos — from elections and governance to corruption, the ICC, and the West Philippine Sea.

This portal is the unified archive of every MCT article ever written — collected from MCT 2.0 on Facebook, the official blog at morningcoffeethoughts.org, and eventually MCT 1.0 (the original Facebook page). All articles, one searchable place.

Founded as a personal blog, MCT has grown into a trusted voice in Philippine political discourse, with a community of over 48,000 followers on Facebook. The writing style is direct, thoroughly sourced, and unapologetically honest.

Beyond politics, MCT also explores personal topics — freelancing, technology, and the everyday experiences of being Filipino in a rapidly changing world. These "Coffee" pieces offer a more personal window into the author's perspective.

Every article aims to cut through noise and provide context that readers can't easily find elsewhere. Sources are cited. Claims are verified. Commentary is clearly labeled as such.

The portal syncs automatically — new articles published on Facebook or the blog appear here within hours.
